About STEMBuddies

Our Story

STEMBuddies was founded by Monica Zhang and Zein Hammad, two first-year university students passionate about creating change and social impact in their community. Experiencing firsthand the negative impact that budget cuts have on the quality of STEM education, they were inspired to find a way to ensure that every student receives equal access to the resources they need to pursue a career in STEM. Through organizing different events and initiatives with STEMBuddies, they hope to empower future innovators across the country.
